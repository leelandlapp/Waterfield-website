City Clerk

The City Clerk plays a vital role in the administration of Waterfield by maintaining official records, overseeing elections, preparing meeting agendas and minutes, and ensuring transparency in local government operations.

The City Clerk is an elected position with a term of four years. The next election for City Clerk is scheduled for November 2026.
