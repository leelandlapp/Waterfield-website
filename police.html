Waterfield Police Department

The Waterfield Police Department is staffed by 4 dedicated officers serving the village to ensure public safety, enforce laws, and maintain community trust. They work closely with residents and other city departments to provide a safe environment for all.

Services include patrol, investigations, community outreach, and emergency response. The department welcomes community involvement through neighborhood watch programs and public safety education.

For non-emergency inquiries or assistance, contact the department at police@waterfieldmi.gov or call (555) 123-4567.
