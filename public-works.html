Waterfield Government

Department of Public Works (DPW)

The Waterfield Department of Public Works is responsible for maintaining the village’s infrastructure including roads, sidewalks, water systems, sewer systems, and public facilities. The department also handles snow removal, street cleaning, and waste management to ensure a safe and clean community.

Established in 1965, the DPW has grown to maintain over 30 miles of streets and sidewalks, delivering high quality services year-round. They work closely with other departments and contractors to manage utilities, perform emergency repairs, and maintain public spaces.

Additional DPW Services

The DPW is also responsible for contracting additional sidewalk plowing services during severe winters to ensure walkways remain safe for residents. They coordinate with local contractors to augment their fleet when necessary.

Regular street maintenance includes pothole repair, signage replacement, and storm drain cleaning to prevent flooding. The DPW works year-round to maintain Waterfield’s infrastructure and respond quickly to emergencies.
