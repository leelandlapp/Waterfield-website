Water & Sewer Department

Department History

The Waterfield Water & Sewer Department was founded in 1965 to address the growing needs of the village’s population for safe drinking water and effective wastewater management.

In 1975, the village constructed its first Water Treatment Plant, a milestone that dramatically improved water quality and supply reliability. The plant has undergone several upgrades, with major modernization in 2015 to incorporate advanced filtration and disinfection technologies.

Over the years, the department has expanded its sewer collection system to cover the entire village, ensuring environmental compliance and public health protection. The team continuously works to maintain, monitor, and improve Waterfield’s water infrastructure.
