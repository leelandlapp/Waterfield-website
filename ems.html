Waterfield Emergency Medical Service (EMS)

The Waterfield EMS provides critical pre-hospital emergency care and medical transport for residents and visitors. Staffed by six trained members, the department ensures rapid response and compassionate care 24/7.

EMS Department non-emergency phone: (555) 123-7890 — for emergencies dial 911

Founded in 1985, Waterfield EMS has grown steadily to serve the community with modern equipment and professional staff committed to public health and safety.
