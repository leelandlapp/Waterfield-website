Departments of Waterfield — Fairs and Festivals

The Fairs and Festivals Department organizes and manages the annual celebrations, community fairs, parades, and cultural festivals in Waterfield. These events bring together residents and visitors to celebrate the village’s heritage, foster community spirit, and support local artisans and businesses.

From the Spring Flower Festival to the Harvest Fair, the department coordinates all aspects including permits, vendor booths, entertainment, and safety measures. This department works closely with other city departments such as Public Works, Police, and Parks and Recreation to ensure successful and enjoyable events for all.

For event schedules, volunteer opportunities, and vendor information, please contact the Fairs and Festivals Department at fairs@waterfieldmi.gov or call (555) 123-4567.
