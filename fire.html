Waterfield Fire Department

The Waterfield Fire Department operates as a volunteer fire service dedicated to protecting the community from fire hazards and emergency situations. Our volunteers are trained and ready to respond at any time to keep Waterfield safe.

The department provides fire prevention education, fire safety inspections, and emergency response services. Collaboration with neighboring departments ensures prompt and effective assistance when needed.

For fire safety information or to volunteer, please contact us at fire@waterfieldmi.gov or call (555) 123-4567.
