Waterfield Fire Department

The Waterfield Fire Department is a proud volunteer department with approximately 27 dedicated firefighters serving the community. Founded in 1925, it has a long history of protecting Waterfield residents, responding to emergencies, and promoting fire safety education. Over the decades, the department has grown with new equipment and training to meet modern challenges.

Water Tanker Truck A large tanker truck designed to transport and supply water to fire scenes where hydrants are unavailable. It holds up to 2,500 gallons of water and supports extended firefighting operations. 2017
