Department of Parks and Recreation

The Waterfield Department of Parks and Recreation is dedicated to maintaining and enhancing the village’s parks, recreational facilities, and community programs. Established in 1972, the department has grown to include a seasonal outdoor pool, several park areas, sports leagues, and special community events.

The department emphasizes community wellness, youth engagement, and environmental stewardship through diverse programs and well-maintained public spaces.
