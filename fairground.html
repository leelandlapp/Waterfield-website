Department of Waterfield Fairgrounds

The Waterfield Fairgrounds serve as the central location for community events, fairs, and festivals throughout the year. The department manages the scheduling, maintenance, and facilities to ensure a safe and welcoming environment for residents and visitors alike.

The Fairgrounds include ample parking, outdoor event spaces, exhibition halls, and amenities to accommodate a wide variety of activities. Coordination with local organizations helps to bring diverse events to the village.

For reservations, event planning, or more information, please contact the Fairgrounds Department at fairgrounds@waterfieldmi.gov or call (555) 123-4567.
