Waterfield Department of Water and Sewer

The Waterfield Department of Water and Sewer is responsible for providing clean, safe drinking water and efficient wastewater treatment services to the village residents. Our department maintains the infrastructure, monitors water quality, and ensures compliance with environmental regulations.

We continuously work to improve water distribution systems and sewer networks, aiming to support the health and well-being of the community.

For questions, service requests, or emergencies, please contact the department at watersewer@waterfieldmi.gov or call (555) 123-4567.
