Mayor Cabinet

The Mayor Cabinet serves as the executive team advising the Mayor on policy, community issues, and administrative matters. This team collaborates closely to develop initiatives that support the growth, safety, and well-being of Waterfield.

Members include department heads and key advisors who help coordinate village operations and public programs.
