Village Council

The Village Council consists of 5 Senators, 9 Council members, and 10 House Representatives who oversee the local governance of Waterfield.

Departments

  • Police Department — 4 officers serving Waterfield
  • Fire Department — Community volunteer fire service
  • Public Works — Roads, water, and snow removal
  • Planning and Zoning — Managing growth and land use
