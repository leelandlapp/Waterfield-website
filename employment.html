Join Our Community

Waterfield is growing and we’re always looking for talented and dedicated individuals to join our team. Whether you’re interested in public service, local businesses, education, or community development, there are many opportunities here.

Current Openings

  • Public Works Laborer: Full-time, responsible for maintaining roads, parks, and public facilities. Experience preferred but not required.
  • Police Officer: Entry-level and experienced positions available. Must meet state certification requirements.
  • Administrative Assistant: Support village government offices with clerical duties. Strong organizational skills required.
  • Event Coordinator: Plan and manage festivals, fairs, and community events throughout the year.

How to Apply

Send your resume and cover letter to jobs@waterfieldmi.gov or visit the Village Hall at 123 Main Street for an application.
